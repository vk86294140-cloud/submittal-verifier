SECTION 09 68 13 - TILE CARPETING

PART 1 - GENERAL

1.1 SUMMARY
A. Section includes modular carpet tile and accessories for interior floors.

1.2 SUBMITTALS
A. Product Data: Submit manufacturer's product data for each type of carpet tile,
   adhesive, and accessory specified.
B. Shop Drawings: Submit shop drawings showing tile layout, seam locations, and
   direction of pile.
C. Samples: Submit two samples, minimum 12 inch by 12 inch, of each carpet tile
   in the specified color.
D. Test Reports: Submit certified test reports from a qualified independent
   testing agency indicating compliance with specified performance requirements.
E. Maintenance Data: Submit maintenance data for inclusion in operation and
   maintenance manuals.
F. Warranty: Submit manufacturer's standard warranty.

1.3 QUALITY ASSURANCE
A. Fire-Test-Response Characteristics: Carpet tile shall comply with ASTM E648
   for critical radiant flux of not less than 0.45 watts per square centimeter.
B. Smoke Density: Carpet tile shall comply with ASTM E662 with a maximum smoke
   density of 450.
C. Electrostatic Propensity: Carpet tile shall comply with AATCC 134 with a
   maximum static generation of 3.5 kilovolts.

PART 2 - PRODUCTS

2.1 PERFORMANCE REQUIREMENTS
A. Pile Height: Provide carpet tile with a minimum pile height of 0.27 inch.
B. Tile Size: Provide carpet tile 24 inch by 24 inch nominal.
C. Total Weight: Provide carpet tile with a minimum total weight of 70 ounce per
   square yard.
D. Tufted Pile Yarn: Provide 100 percent solution-dyed nylon yarn.

PART 3 - EXECUTION

3.1 INSTALLATION
A. Install carpet tile in accordance with manufacturer's written instructions and
   CRI 104.
